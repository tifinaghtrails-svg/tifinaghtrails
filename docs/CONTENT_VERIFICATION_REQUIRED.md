# Content Verification Required

The site should not use trust claims in SEO snippets, structured data, ads, or outreach unless Mustapha can provide proof. Keep this file updated as proof is collected.

## Claims To Confirm

| Claim | Current location | Required proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "Certified guides" / "certified local mountain guides" | Home, About, Tours, Booking, Footer, FAQ, email copy | Copies or photos of official guide licenses/certificates |
| "Licensed by the Ministry of Tourism, Morocco" | Footer | Business license or registration reference |
| "10+ years experience" / "over a decade" | About, Footer | Bio timeline, license dates, public profiles, or customer records |
| "500+ travelers" | Home trust bar, About stats | Booking history, platform reviews, or documented customer count |
| "4.9 rating" / review count if used later | Structured data or reviews | Public review source URL and exact count |
| "Free Marrakech hotel pickup and drop-off" | Home, tours, booking | Confirm it is included for all listed tours, or qualify per tour |
| "No hidden fees" / transparent pricing | Booking, FAQ | Confirm all mandatory costs are listed for each tour |
| "24/7 support during your trip" | Booking, custom tour | Confirm real support availability and phone coverage |
| "100% safe and secure booking" | Booking | Avoid absolute safety claims; use factual security/process copy instead |
| Insurance coverage | FAQ | Insurance certificate or policy summary |

## Recommended Copy Rule

If proof is not available, use softer factual copy:

- "Local mountain guides" instead of "certified local mountain guides"
- "Customer-loved treks" instead of "500+ travelers"
- "Clear inclusions listed on every tour" instead of "no hidden fees"
- "Support during your trip" instead of "24/7 support"

## Structured Data Rule

Do not add `aggregateRating`, review stars, certification, or license identifiers to JSON-LD until the proof source is public and stable.
