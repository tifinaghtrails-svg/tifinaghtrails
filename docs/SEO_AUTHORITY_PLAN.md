# SEO Authority Plan

## Principles

- Build authority with real proof, real photos, real customer stories, and useful trekking advice.
- Avoid buying links or publishing fake reviews.
- Prefer a few trustworthy travel references over many low-quality directory links.

## Practical Actions

- Ask partner riads and Marrakech guesthouses to link to the relevant tour page.
- Publish practical guides based on actual customer questions.
- Add original photos with descriptive alt text.
- Collect verified reviews on public platforms.
- Share tour pages on official social profiles.
- Build a simple "press and partners" list only when real partners exist.

## Content That Can Earn Links

- Mount Toubkal packing checklist
- Toubkal weather and season guide
- Imlil village visitor guide
- High Atlas trekking difficulty guide
- Family-friendly Atlas day trip guide

## Avoid

- Fake awards
- Fake certification badges
- AI-generated review pages
- Hidden text or keyword stuffing
- Links from unrelated spam directories
